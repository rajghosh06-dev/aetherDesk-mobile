# User Guide

Welcome to the **AetherDesk Mobile** User Guide. This manual details how to navigate the app to seamlessly scan, organize, and export your physical documents.

## Using the Scanner
1. Open the application. On the main dashboard, tap the **Camera/Scan** button to invoke the Document Scanner.
2. The scanner will utilize Google ML Kit. Position the document inside the frame; the application will hardware-accelerate edge detection and auto-capture the document (or you can capture it manually).
3. **Important:** To ensure peak performance and stability, batch scanning is limited to a maximum of 38 pages at once. 

## Organizing Scans
- Once your scans are captured, you'll be taken to the **Export Screen**.
- **Reordering:** Simply press, hold, and drag any thumbnail to rearrange the pages. The app utilizes highly optimized drag-and-drop algorithms to ensure snappy and accurate reordering.
- **Removing Pages:** Click the `'x'` on the top right of any image to smoothly remove it from the batch. 

## Exporting & Sharing
The Export interface allows you to choose exactly how your documents leave the app:

### 1. File Type Selection
Choose between exporting your scans as a **PDF Document** or as **Individual Photos (Images)**. The file size estimation handles exact memory calculations on the fly so you know exactly how large the export will be.

### 2. Export Destinations
You have native integration with Android's secure sharing ecosystem. Simply tap one of the officially branded buttons:
- **Share to WhatsApp:** Immediately packages and shares your PDF or sequential images directly into a WhatsApp conversation.
- **Save to Google Drive:** Initiates a direct upload to your connected Google account.
- **Save to OneDrive:** Passes your documents straight to Microsoft OneDrive.
- **Save to Downloads:** Generates the file natively and pushes a push-notification. Clicking the notification will open the saved file in your device's default File Manager!
